Posted on 3/6/18

Often a problem for many people weather your in Middle School, Highschool, College, a graduate,
40 years old or a senior citizen we usually ask ourselves what our individual purpose is or was on the
people around us, suprisingly the answer might shock you.

From every smile to every frown your actions play a vital role on your personal impact to society
and the people around you. You might not realize it's the small actions you do that have the greatest
impact. If you walk down the street and give someone of less fortune a dollar it could change that
persons whole day and maybe even their own perception of society
